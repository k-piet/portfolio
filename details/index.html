CAPSTONE: Local Hazards Network

Interdisciplinary

The Local Hazards Network project is my 2026 ANU Capstone Project, assessing the feasibility of multi-hazard environmental monitoring in rural New South Wales using low-power LoRa sensor nodes and the MeshCore communication protocol. Funded by the Australian Government National Emergency Management Agency (NEMA) through the Disaster Relief Fund (DRF), we're building disaster resilience for at-risk communities.

My roles within the project so far are the power systems designer and repository manager. Under those roles, I am responsible for electrical safety, implementing power generation and storage into the modules, and ensuring that our project documentation is consistent, accessible, and up to industry strandards.
